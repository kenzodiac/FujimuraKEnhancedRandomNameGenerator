This is an app that lets you manage a list of names.

You can complie the list yourself, add a preset list of CodeStack Academy students, do a little of both, and add/delete list entries on command. The list will still be here, even if you refresh the app!

You can also sort the list into randomized groups; the size of those groups being up to you.

There's also a clear list button - don't press that button unless you want to wipe the list entirely and start from scratch. You've been warned!

Enjoy!
